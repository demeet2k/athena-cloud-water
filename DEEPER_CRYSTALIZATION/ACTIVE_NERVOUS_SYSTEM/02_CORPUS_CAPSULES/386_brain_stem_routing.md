## Action Alphabet

Nine constitutional actions, strictly ordered:

1. `REFUSE_INADMISSIBLE` — Omega denial or scope inadmissibility
2. `QUARANTINE` — forbidden contradiction or unresolved-failure classes
3. `REPLAY_FIRST` — replay dependency is the primary gap
4. `REQUEST_WITNESSES` — truth burden is the real blocker
5. `REQUEST_HELP` — external dependency or owner help gap
6. `ESCALATE_TO_COMMITTEE` — stewardship or branch-limit law fires
7. `COMPRESS_TO_SEED` — forward motion blocked but continuation value alive
8. `HOLD` — lawful but below activation threshold
9. `ACTIVATE_NOW` — cleared legality and threshold gates
