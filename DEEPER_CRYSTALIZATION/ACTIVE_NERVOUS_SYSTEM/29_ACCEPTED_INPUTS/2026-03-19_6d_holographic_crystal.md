## Core Architecture

The 6D Holographic Crystal is the octahedral-geometry hologram that extends the 4D compression seed (H4) into a full six-dimensional crystal body. Where H4 = (K, Pi, Z, Lambda, Psi) defines the 4D seed, the 6D crystal provides the spatial-geometric framework in which all six dimensions become simultaneously navigable.

### Six Vertices (Octahedral Body)

The 6D crystal has six vertices V1-V6 arranged as an octahedron:
- V1/V6: polar axis (Z-axis)
- V2/V4: equatorial X-axis
- V3/V5: equatorial Y-axis

### Fifteen Edge Pairs

The octahedron generates 15 unordered vertex pairs (edges):
G12, G13, G14, G15, G16, G23, G24, G25, G26, G34, G35, G36, G45, G46, G56

These edges are the holographic channels through which dimensional information propagates between vertices.

### Generator Algebra

The rotation generators satisfy SO(3) algebra:
- [E_alpha, E_beta] = E_gamma
- [E_beta, E_gamma] = E_alpha
- [E_gamma, E_alpha] = E_beta

Radial and scale generators commute with each other. The full algebra is the semi-direct product of SO(3) with the radial-scale group.
